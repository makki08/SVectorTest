The Online Class Record for FEU – East Asia College ITE Teaching Associates is propose in replacement of the current Class Record Spreadsheet of the college for it to have an organized flow of transaction and an ease of work especially to the  teaching associates of FEU – East Asia College. The study is concerned on how the current system performs throughout the academe year.
Generation of reports that the current spreadsheet covers such as updating, and viewing the grade book for each course and/or student records, generating the student reports are commending the current spreadsheet to be inefficient for the needs of the institution.
Since the study involves proper records handling, tracing the constraints and evaluation of the current spreadsheet is essential. Thus, the proponents gathered necessary data that will help in the progress of enhancing the existing system of FEU – East Asia College.
